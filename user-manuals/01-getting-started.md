# 01. Getting Started

Welcome to **my-ob1**, your personal AI-powered cognitive data layer. This system captures your thoughts, structures them, and uses them to help you achieve your goals.

## The Core Personas
When you interact with **my-ob1**, it acts as three roles simultaneously:

*   **📦 The Planner** — Watches for action items and extracts tasks with inferred deadlines.
*   **🧩 The Strategist** — Maps out a Knowledge Graph of people, projects, and concepts from your context.
*   **🧭 The Mentor** — Evaluates every thought against your stored goals/preferences and surfaces strategic insights.

## How it works
Your primary interaction with my-ob1 is through **Slack** or an **MCP-compatible AI client**.
The system silently processes your inputs, extracts valuable structures (tasks, entities, concepts), and saves them into a connected Knowledge Graph on Supabase.

*   You capture unstructured thoughts in Slack.
*   The system uses LLMs to parse and organize your thoughts into structured memory.
*   It automatically compares your inputs against your declared goals to provide mentorship and insights.
*   You use your AI assistants (like Claude, Cursor, or Antigravity) hooked up to the **MCP Server** to recall information, search your memory, and update tasks.

To learn more about putting data into the system, continue to [Capturing Thoughts](./02-capturing-thoughts.md).
